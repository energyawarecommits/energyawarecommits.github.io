## A Large-Scale Study of Energy-Aware Commits This page contains pointers to all the primary data we used to conduct our study on energy-aware commits, please refer to them below. ## [Analysis Spreadsheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1n9-FGtfvP1PIV25s3w0YWayzwGKx4ETYNMHc5ej4O4I/edit?usp=sharing) ## This spreadsheet is a public Google Spreadsheet document that holds all the results of our analysis. It includes the following sheets: - __Dashboard__: contains aggregated data about overall statistics discussed in the paper. - __Commit Data__: contains the bulk of the commits data and it also contains the final theme assigned to each commits. - __Survey Template__: contains both templates of the surveys sent to commits authors. - __Survey Data__: contains the data collected from from the surveys. ## Authors - __Irineu Moura__: imlm2@cin.ufpe.br - __Wellington Oliveira__: woj@cin.ufpe.br - __Gustavo Pinto__: gpinto@ufpa.br - __Felipe Ebert__: fe@cin.ufpe.br - __Fernando Castor__: castor@cin.ufpe.br
